Payment Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Thank you for choosing The CREST Collaborative for services. This Payment Policy outlines our terms regarding fees, payment methods, and related procedures.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept payment via:

  • PayPal: Secure online payment processing
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Via PayPal (no PayPal account required)
  • Other methods: Please contact us to discuss alternative payment arrangements

Make a Payment:

Pay via PayPal

2. Service Fees

Service fees are determined based on the scope and type of services provided:

  • Personal HR Advisor Services: Rates provided during initial consultation
  • Organizational Consultation: Available by monthly retainer or custom pricing based on project
  • The Resilience Lab Experiences: Available for Community Meet Ups and Retreat Experiences
  • Subscription Services: Rates provided at time of subscription
  • Package Options: Available for multi-session engagements
  • Custom Pricing: Available for specialized projects or extended engagements

All fees will be discussed and agreed upon before services begin. You will receive a clear breakdown of costs via email confirmation.

3. Payment Terms

  • Initial Payment: Payment is due before or at the time of your first session
  • Package Services: Full or partial payment may be required upfront, as agreed
  • Ongoing Services: Payment is due at the beginning of each billing period
  • Invoices: Sent via email and due within 7 days of receipt unless otherwise specified

4. Late Payments

Payment is expected by the due date specified on your invoice. If payment is not received within 7 days of the due date:

  • Services may be paused until payment is received
  • Scheduled sessions may be rescheduled or cancelled
  • A late fee of $25 may be applied to overdue invoices

5. Cancellation & Rescheduling

Session Cancellations:

  • 24+ Hours Notice: Full credit or reschedule, no charge
  • Less than 24 Hours: 50% of session fee charged
  • No-Show: Full session fee charged

Rescheduling: Sessions may be rescheduled at no charge with at least 24 hours notice.

6. Refund Policy

  • Single Sessions: Refunds are not provided once a session is completed
  • Packages: Refunds for unused sessions may be provided on a case-by-case basis, minus any sessions already completed
  • Unused Services: If you decide to discontinue services, refunds will be prorated based on services rendered
  • Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days

To request a refund, please contact info@thecrestcollaborative.com with your invoice number and reason for the request.

7. Payment Security

Your payment information is secure:

  • All payments are processed through PayPal's secure payment system
  • We do not store your credit card or banking information
  • All transactions are encrypted and comply with industry security standards

8. Disputes

If you have questions or concerns about a charge, please contact us immediately at info@thecrestcollaborative.com. We are committed to resolving any payment issues promptly and fairly.

Please allow us the opportunity to address your concern before initiating a chargeback or dispute through your payment provider.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Payment Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
